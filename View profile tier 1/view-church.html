About Aroma International

Welcome to Aroma International! We are a vibrant, Christ-centered community dedicated to spreading God's love and transforming lives through the Gospel. For over 20 years, our church has been a beacon of hope in the community, offering worship services, Bible studies, counseling, and extensive outreach programs. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where people from all walks of life can encounter God's love, grow in faith, and discover their purpose. Led by Pastor David Emmanuel, our congregation of 2,500+ members is committed to serving both our local community and global missions. We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of Scripture, and the transformative work of the Holy Spirit in every believer's life.
