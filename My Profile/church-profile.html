About Me

Dedicated servant of God with over 20 years of ministry experience, leading Grace Community Church with a vision to transform lives through the Gospel. My journey began with a calling to serve marginalized communities, which has grown into a ministry that reaches thousands weekly through worship services, Bible studies, counseling, and community outreach programs. I hold a Master of Divinity from Trinity Seminary and have authored several books on practical Christian living. Our church family is committed to creating a welcoming space where people from all walks of life can encounter God's love, grow in faith, and discover their purpose. Beyond the pulpit, I'm passionate about mentoring young leaders, supporting local charities, and building bridges between different faith communities. My wife Sarah and I have been blessed with three children, and together we strive to model Christ's love in our community and beyond.
