Important Terms & Conditions

Upfront Payment Required

The full campaign budget (10,000 ETB) will be deducted from your account immediately upon creation. This amount is transferred directly to Astegni and allocated to your campaign.

Cancel Anytime

You can cancel your campaign at any time. Campaigns that have spent less than 100,000 ETB will incur a 5% cancellation fee on the remaining budget. Campaigns that spent 100,000 ETB or more can cancel with no fee.

Pause Campaign Anytime (No Fee)

You can pause your campaign at any time without any fee. Pausing temporarily stops ad delivery while preserving your remaining budget. Resume anytime to continue your campaign. Note: Minimum campaign budget is 10,000 ETB.

Fair & Transparent Billing

You'll receive a detailed transaction for every 1,000 impressions charged. View your complete billing history in your account transactions.

Real-Time Analytics

Track impressions, clicks, conversions, and spending in real-time. Access detailed performance reports anytime from your campaign dashboard.
