Stop Anytime - Pay for What You Use

You can stop your campaign at any time. You'll only pay for impressions actually delivered. If you stop early, a 2% cancellation fee applies to the unspent budget. 20% payment is non-refundable but covers your first impressions.

Pause Anytime (No Fee)

You can pause your campaign at any time without any fee or settlement. Pausing temporarily stops ad delivery. Resume anytime to continue where you left off. Only settle up when you stop the campaign permanently.

Invoice on Payment

You receive an invoice each time you make a payment — not after impressions are delivered. Each invoice shows the amount paid, cost per impression, and your running balance. Impression delivery and spending are tracked in real time on your campaign dashboard.
