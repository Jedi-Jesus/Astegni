### Executive Summary

Astegni is an educational technology platform with **two IP-protected innovations** that address a critical $200B global market gap: **trust and quality in private tutoring**.

**The Problem**: Parents worldwide struggle to find quality tutors, relying on unverified billboards, agents, and social media—forcing them to accept tutors blindly.

**Our Solution**: A verified tutor marketplace with a **5-factor rating system**, powered by proprietary **Digital Whiteboard** and **Digital Lab** technologies.

**Current Traction**:
- ✅ 17,000+ Ethiopian tutors in database
- ✅ Digital Whiteboard (Phase 1) production-ready
- ✅ Validated market need through 50+ parent interviews
- ✅ Beta launch: 500+ users in Addis Ababa

**Market Opportunity**:
- **Ethiopia**: 25M students, growing private tutoring market
- **Africa**: 500M students across 54 countries
- **Global**: 1.5B students, $200B tutoring market

**Funding Request**: $500K - $1M Seed Funding
