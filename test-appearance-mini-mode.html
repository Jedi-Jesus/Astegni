What is Mini-Mode?

Mini-mode transforms the appearance modal into a compact widget positioned at the bottom-right of your screen. This allows you to preview theme changes in real-time without the modal blocking your view of the page content.

Features

  • Live theme preview while modal is minimized
  • Bottom-right positioning for unobstructed view
  • Quick access to theme and color palette settings
  • Click header to expand back to full mode
  • Smooth animations and transitions
  • Shows only essential controls in compact view

How to Test Mini-Mode:

  1. Click the button above to open the appearance modal
  2. Look for the minimize button (â†“) in the top-right of the modal header
  3. Click the minimize button to enter mini-mode
  4. The modal will shrink and move to the bottom-right corner
  5. Try changing themes or color palettes to see live previews
  6. Click the modal header to expand back to full mode
  7. Click "Save Changes" to apply your selections
