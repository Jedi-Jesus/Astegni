## Color Scheme

### Stats Cards:
- **Average Rating**: `text-yellow-600` (Gold)
- **Subject Matter**: `text-blue-600` (Blue)
- **Communication**: `text-green-600` (Green)
- **Punctuality**: `text-purple-600` (Purple)

### Filter Tabs:
- **Default**: White background, gray border
- **Hover**: Light background, blue border, slight shadow
- **Active**: Blue gradient background, white text

### Review Cards:
- **Background**: `var(--card-bg)` (Theme-aware)
- **Border**: Left border (gray default, blue on hover)
- **Text**: `var(--text-primary)` (Theme-aware)

### 4-Factor Badges:
- **Subject Matter**: Blue background (`bg-blue-100`, `text-blue-700`)
- **Communication**: Green background (`bg-green-100`, `text-green-700`)
- **Punctuality**: Purple background (`bg-purple-100`, `text-purple-700`)
- **Discipline**: Orange background (`bg-orange-100`, `text-orange-700`)

---

## Animations & Transitions

### Filter Tabs:
- `transition: all 0.3s ease`
- Hover: `transform: translateY(-2px)`
- Active: No transform (stays in place)

### Review Cards:
- `transition: all 0.3s ease`
- Hover: `transform: translateY(-2px)` + enhanced shadow

### Tooltip:
- `animation: tooltipFadeIn 0.2s ease-in-out`
- Fade in from slightly above
- No exit animation (instant remove)

### No Carousel:
- âŒ Removed all opacity fade-in/out
- âŒ Removed all interval-based animations
- âœ… Static, scrollable list
