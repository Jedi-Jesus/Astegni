About the Author

Sarah Mitchell is an award-winning author of contemporary fiction and literary non-fiction. With over 20 published works spanning two decades, she has captivated readers worldwide with her profound storytelling and unique voice. Her work explores themes of identity, belonging, and the human experience, earning her numerous accolades including the prestigious National Book Award and the Pulitzer Prize for Fiction.
