About Me

Award-winning investigative journalist with over 15 years of experience covering technology, culture, and social innovation on a global scale. My work has been featured in major publications worldwide, including The New York Times, The Guardian, and The Washington Post. Specializing in long-form investigative reporting, I've uncovered stories that have led to policy changes and corporate accountability. My documentary "Digital Divide" won the International Press Freedom Award in 2022. Currently working on a book about the intersection of AI and journalism while continuing to report on breaking news and emerging technologies. Fluent in four languages, I've reported from conflict zones, tech hubs, and remote communities across 127 countries, always committed to bringing truth to light and giving voice to the unheard.
